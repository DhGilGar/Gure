RECOVERY OF EXCISE DUTY ON PROFESSIONAL FUEL
WHO CAN REQUEST IT?
Vehicles used for the road transport of goods with a maximum authorised weight of 7.5 tonnes or more.
Motor vehicles used for regular or occasional passenger transport, included in categories M2 or M3 established in Council Directive 70/156/EEC of 6 February 1970.
HOW MUCH IT CAN BE RECOVERED?
Professional Diesel Recovery (SPAIN)
  • Refund applicable to both goods and passenger transport.
  • 4.9 cents/litre can be recovered.
  • Not Retroactively applied. Recovery possible from the date of registration of the company.
T.I.C.P.E. Recovery (France)
  • Refund applicable to both freight and passenger transport.
  • 15.7 cents/litre can be recovered.
  • Retroactively applied for 2 years.
Recovery of DROIT D’ACCISE SPÉCIA (BELGIUM)
  • Refund applicable to road transport of goods only.
  • 24.7 cents/litre can be recovered.
  • Retroactively applied for 3 years if the company has been registered for 3 years.
