Bech & ABC Flytning

Bech & ABC Flytteforretning in Kolding is one of the companies that use the services of Guretruck, a company specialized in fleet management, tachograph management, legal services, and calculation of minimum wages. It offers these services to transport companies in the European Union that travel to different countries for logistical reasons..

“Without Guretruck's help, I wouldn't drive overseas at all. There are too many rules that require drivers to notify and document the Minimum Wage, something reasonably difficult, because the regulations vary from one country to another. Guretruck incorporates a tool for calculating the Minimum Wage, which helps us easily register in each of the countries. We do it all from one place and you make sure you meet the individual requirements of each country.”

“On the platform we can check what the Minimum Wage is for each country through which we are going to drive. And then we just have to match it, something relatively simple with Danish salaries ” - he says and points out that they have never had problems.

“When we make a stop and review the data on site, something we do from time to time, everything is in order”

"The platform may seem complicated at first, but once you work with it a couple of times it's not hard to understand." - he comments.

“You can use TruckController for many things, and even have the App on your IPad. Our drivers can have this app on their phones. But to cover our needs, we have chosen to carry the printed documentation in a ring binder. Not everyone is a computer geek” – he points out, giving a few more examples of what has led him to collaboration with Guretruck.

He can translate documents from Danish to French.

“When new rules come up we are informed, for example it has recently become a requirement in France that we have a work contract/employment agreement. In French! What company can do that? Well, the Guretruck platform does it. We can upload the contract in Danish to the platform, and we automatically have the version translated into French”.

Bech & ABC Flytning also have received help with the fines.

“We had a strange case in England, where we were repeatedly fined for driving on a small 5km stretch of motorway, 11,000 crowns (€1,478.60). I didn't know exactly why we had been sanctioned, we had driven the same section many times. Guretruck's English lawyer examined the sanction and confirmed that it was strange. He contacted us and told us to throw away the tickets. I haven't heard anything else about that case."

Not surprisingly, Michael Jespersen is happy about the collaboration with Guretruck.
